Office Lens
Capture images of whiteboards and documents to make them into readable learning resources.

Office Lens puts a scanner in your pocket. Frame your shot from any angle, and like magic, it digitises notes from whiteboards or physical documents for you to easily tidy up. Using OCR, printed text will automatically be recognised and by converting to Word or PDF, static images can be turned into searchable, editable documents. Never lose your sticky notes again!

Example Activities

Enhance

Share images of whiteboard notes with students, or encourage them to take their own to use as a revision resource.

Empower

Encourage students to take pictures of their written notes. Use as a starting point for wider research and add to more detailed, typed notes.

Extend

Using their captured notes, ask students to create quizzes to test each other's knowledge and understanding.
